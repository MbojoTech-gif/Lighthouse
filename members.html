Wycliff Oiro

Bass Guitarist

Wycliff is an experienced bass player with over 10 years in gospel music ministry. His deep understanding of rhythm and harmony provides the foundation for our group's sound. He's dedicated to using his musical talents to inspire and uplift congregations.

When not performing, Wycliff mentors young musicians in the community and leads worship at his local church.

Sarah Johnson

Lead Vocalist

Sarah brings soulful harmonies and a powerful stage presence to our group. With a background in classical training and gospel music, her voice adds depth and emotion to every performance.

She has been featured on several gospel albums and leads worship workshops across the country.
